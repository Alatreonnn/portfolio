Pastry Chef

My independent first person simulation game made with Unity which the player role-play a traditional Chinese pastry chef and experience the whole pastry-making progress.

The game is inspired by my father-- he loves the fluffy traditional hand-made steamed buns from Northwest China, but due to the low efficiency of manual fermentation, this technique is becoming a lost art. I carefully researched the production process and created this game to document this traditional culture.

Drone Simulater

My independent third person drone game made with Unity. The player needs to operate a drone, take a pictureb of the certain building and bring it back to the start point.

This game borns to serve as a media background for scientific education in a survey. For this purpose, I designed an intuitive flow and optimized the operating system to retain the tactile feel of a drone while making it as simple as possible.

UE5 Combat Demo

Master project of GT Digital Media program. This work aims to reduce the overall difficulty of Souls-like Games. This project integrates the learning of combat mechanics into the narrative experience and offers two distinct combat approaches that players can learn naturally and switch between freely. The theory framework behind refers to Immersion Theory, Flow Theory and Affect Theory.
